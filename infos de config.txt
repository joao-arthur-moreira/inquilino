Configuração do datasource no arquivo context do tomcat

<Resource name="jdbc/brewerDB" auth="Container" 
            factory="org.apache.naming.factory.BeanFactory"
            type="com.mchange.v2.c3p0.ComboPooledDataSource"
            driverClass="org.postgresql.Driver"
            jdbcUrl="jdbc:postgresql://localhost:5432/brewer"
            user="postgres"
            password="sc0rp10n" 
            initialPoolSize="5" 
            minPoolSize="5"
            maxPoolSize="5" 
     />


Colocar os .jar abaixo na pasta lib do tomcat:

c3p0-0.9.5.2
mchange-commons-java-0.2.11
postgresql-9.3-1100-jdbc4
